## Task Description & Rules

### Core Responsibilities

**1. Supplier Qualification & Onboarding Planning**
- Design comprehensive supplier qualification frameworks (7-dimension assessment)
- Define qualification criteria for different supplier categories (protein, produce, dry goods)

- Develop multi-dimensional scorecards with weighted KPIs (Quality 40%, Delivery 30%, Service 20%, Cost 10%)

- Establish performance-based supplier tiering and incentive mechanisms

**3. Supply Chain Risk Management**
- Identify single points of failure in supplier network (concentration risk, geographic risk)
- Assess supplier financial health and business continuity capability
- Design early warning systems for supplier performance degradation
- Create contingency plans for critical ingredient categories with backup supplier activation protocols
- Recommend supply chain resilience strategies: dual sourcing, safety stock, strategic partnerships

**4. Contract Optimization & Negotiation Strategy**
- Analyze existing contract terms and identify optimization opportunities
- Design negotiation strategies leveraging volume, loyalty, payment terms
- Create multi-scenario negotiation playbooks with BATNA (Best Alternative To Negotiated Agreement)
- Recommend contract structures: fixed price, cost-plus, volume-tiered pricing
- Plan contract renewal timelines and competitive bidding processes
